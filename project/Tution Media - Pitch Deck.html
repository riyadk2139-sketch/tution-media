08 · Location privacy
Privacy-controlled location reveal

A guardian's address is never exposed to strangers.

01 · Posted
Fuzzed publicly
Tutors see area, landmark, and an approximate pin. Enough to estimate commute, not enough to find the home.
02 · Requested
One-tap approval
Guardian grants access to one tutor — or all shortlisted at once. Scoped to that single job.
03 · Unlocked
Exact pin + Google Maps
Turn-by-turn navigation in-app. Auto-revoked if not hired. Logged for safety.
Why it matters
Three features in one — safety, anti-leakage (navigation lives in-app), and a trust signal for anxious guardians.

09 · The moat
Anti-disintermediation

We don't lock you in. We make staying better than leaving.

Recurring auto-collection
Removes the awkward monthly "please pay me" ask. Tutors get paid on time, every time.
Payment protection
Attendance-linked guarantee — only available on-platform. Cash deal? You're on your own.
Verified reputation
Accrues from confirmed, paid tuitions. The asset they'd forfeit by going informal.
Replacement guarantee
Not happy with the trial? Free replacement. Removes the default to the informal market.
Masked comms
No raw phone number exchange. Safety + anti-leakage in one mechanism.
Verified income proof
Formal receipts the informal market can't produce. Basis for future lending products.

Phasing

V1 · MVP
NOW → Q3
